Our first edition of *Anthologies*, which we will publish on the web, will be on the theme of ***Beginnings***. 

The deadline for submitting pieces to the zine is **30 September 2026**. We will curate and prepare the zine in the weeks following the deadline.

Here are a few ways you could interpret this edition’s theme:

- How do you know when you are ready to start something new?
- How did you feel when you first started doing something new? This could be learning a new skill, going travelling, volunteering – whatever applies to you.
- What do you think of when you hear the word “beginning”?
- Have you ever started something new and realised it wasn’t for you? Was that moment a stepping stone toward something bigger and better?
- When does your day begin? How does your day begin?
- How do beginnings relate to ends? Does one thing end and a new thing begin, or is there an intermediary moment where something is both ending and something is beginning?
- Imagine a character who feels they need a new beginning. What did the character used to do? Who do they want to be?
- Imagine a character who has just started something new and feels lost. How do they grow through their initial anxieties?
- Poetic prompt: When does a song begin?

We have a [contribution guide](/anthologies/contribute) set up to help you with submitting your writings. You can also discuss with the community on [our new forum](/forum).
